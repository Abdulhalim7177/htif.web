Our Mission

Innovate

Support the development of pioneering health technologies that address pressing healthcare challenges.

Collaborate

Build a strong network of multidisciplinary professionals, researchers, and institutions to foster innovation.

Educate

Promote continuous learning, capacity-building, and awareness of emerging health technologies.

Advocate

Influence policies, systems, and initiatives that promote ethical and responsible use of digital and emerging technologies in healthcare.

Strategic Goals

  • To empower young innovators (18â€“35) with the knowledge, skills, and tools needed to develop health technology solutions.
  • To strengthen collaboration between health and technology sectors through events, hackathons, and innovation challenges.
  • To promote research, innovation, and entrepreneurship in health technology across Nigeria and Africa.
  • To support the development and adoption of scalable, affordable, and impactful digital health innovations.
  • To build a sustainable ecosystem of youth-led health tech innovators contributing to national and global health goals.
  • To engage policymakers and stakeholders in promoting technology-driven healthcare reforms.

Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Collaboration & Networking

Excellence & Professionalism

Membership Eligibility

Join a community of passionate health tech innovators

Age Requirement

Must be between 18 and 35 years old at the time of application

Passion & Interest

Demonstrate interest in health, technology, innovation, or entrepreneurship

Background

Open to individuals from diverse fields, including:

  • Healthcare (Medicine, Nursing, Public Health, Lab Science, Pharmacy, etc.)
  • Engineering & ICT
  • Data Science & AI
  • Software Development
  • Research & Academia
  • Business, Innovation & Entrepreneurship
  • Youth leaders passionate about digital health

Ethical Conduct

Must uphold HTiF's values of ethics, professionalism, inclusivity, and respect

Active Participation

Willing to participate in programs, workshops, trainings, and community projects
